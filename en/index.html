Who we are

A community guided by the Word, prayer, and mission.

Light & Grace Ministries exists to glorify God by proclaiming the gospel of Jesus Christ, making disciples, strengthening families, and cooperating with churches and leaders to reach new communities.

“Seeing it is God who said, ‘Light will shine out of darkness,’ who has shone in our hearts…” — 2 Corinthians 4:6, WEB

Our beliefs

Biblical convictions with a cooperative mission.

We affirm the historic teachings of biblical Christianity and identify doctrinally with the Baptist Faith and Message 2000.

  • The Bible is the Word of God and our authority.
  • There is one God: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • Salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ.
  • The church proclaims the gospel, makes disciples, and serves its neighbors.
